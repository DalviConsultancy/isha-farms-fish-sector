Guppy Care: The Basics

Once your guppies are settled, their daily care is straightforward. Feed them a high-quality flake food once or twice a day, only what they can consume in a minute or two. Overfeeding can lead to poor water quality. Perform a 25% water change weekly to keep the water fresh and clean. Regularly observe your guppies for any signs of stress or illness, ensuring they remain vibrant and active.

Tank Setup: Quick Start

Setting up a beautiful home for your guppies is easier than you think. For a small group of 3-5 guppies, a 15-20 litre tank is a great start. Just add clean water (dechlorinated with a simple water conditioner), a small filter, and some gravel. Let the filter run for a day or two, and you're ready to introduce your new friends!
